  • Numbered Headings options

    Comma separated: H3, H5

    Custom number format

    Define custom formatting per heading. Note: empty options will not render anything.  Formatting options.

    Formatting options

    • decimal
    • iso-2145
    • full-decimal
    • lower-latin
    • lower-roman
    • lower-greek
    • upper-latin
    • upper-roman
    • upper-greek
    • custom

    Examples

    Heading 1

    Chapter [h1.decimal]

    Results in Chapter 1, Chapter 2, etc.

    Heading 2

    [h1.decimal].[h2.upper-latin]

    Results in 1.A, 1.B, 2.A, 2.B, etc.
